Controls

  • click - create a particle
  • click + drag - create a particle with velocity
  • shift + drag - pan the camera

  • up - double mass
  • down - halve mass
  • left - decrease gravity
  • right - increase gravity
  • f - follow the largest particle
  • v - show velocities

  • h - hide/show this panel
